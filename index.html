About Us

We promise more than pixels. We promise emotion, authenticity, and artistry. Our lens captures the essence of weddings, the spirit of celebrations, and the soul of landscapes. From Vadodara to London, we’ve woven a tapestry of memories.

Who We Are

At ShotByParth, we believe that every photograph tells a story—a moment frozen in time, waiting to be cherished forever. Our journey began in the vibrant streets of Vadodara, where passion met the lens. From there, we embarked on an artistic adventure that led us to the heart of London, where creativity knows no bounds.

Why We Do This

Our vision is simple yet profound: to transform ordinary scenes into extraordinary memories. Whether it’s a candid portrait, a breathtaking landscape, or a joyous celebration, we infuse each frame with emotion, authenticity, and artistry. Our lens becomes a brush, and every click paints a masterpiece.
